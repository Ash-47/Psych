Conscientiousness

Standard features of this dimension include high levels of thoughtfulness, good impulse control, and goal-directed behaviors. Highly conscientious people tend to be organized and mindful of details. They plan ahead, think about how their behavior affects others, and are mindful of deadlines.

The following table suggests a contrast between those with high and low levels of conscientiousness:


High Low
Spends time preparing Dislikes structures and schedules
Finishes important tasks right away Messes up and doesn't care much
Pays attention to detail Sort of careless
Always follows a strict schedule Procrastinates important tasks
